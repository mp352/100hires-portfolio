## 🚀 Part 2: Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS (Research Project)

### Why This Topic?
Cold outbound infrastructure and pipeline velocity are critical for B2B SaaS growth. This project focuses on gathering high-signal tactics from real practitioners regarding deliverability, multi-channel approach, and high-conversion copywriting.

### Expert Selection Criteria
Instead of picking generic search engine results, I curated 10 experts who are active practitioners, founders of outreach software (Lemlist, LeadMagic), or agencies actively running campaigns today.

### What Was Collected
*   **/research/sources.md**: A complete annotated bibliography of the 10 chosen experts.
*   **/research/linkedin-posts/**: Deep-dives into deliverability scaling (Guillaume Moubeche), programmatic prospecting (Eric Nowoslawski), and anti-spam copy (Josh Braun).
*   **/research/youtube-transcripts/**: Technical breakdowns of technical domain setup (Nick Abraham) and template optimization (Alex Berman).
