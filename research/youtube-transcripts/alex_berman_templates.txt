[Alex Berman - The 3 Golden Rules of Cold Email Templates]
Transcript Excerpt:
"Most people fail at cold email because their templates look like essays. Rule number one: It must be shorter than 4 sentences. Rule number two: It must focus heavily on the case study. Give them a specific metric—how you helped a similar company increase revenue by 30%. Rule number three: Make the next step frictionless. Don't ask them for a meeting, just ask if they want a short 2-minute video explaining the strategy..."
