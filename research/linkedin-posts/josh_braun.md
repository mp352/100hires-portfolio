## Topic: The Anti-Spam Copywriting Framework
- Never start a cold email talking about your product or features. Start with a "Permission-Based Hook" or a relevant problem observation.
- Keep the entire email under 75 words. The goal is to get a reply, not to close a sale in the first touchpoint.
- Use a soft Call to Action (CTA): "Open to exploring how we did this?" instead of "Are you free for a 30-min call on Tuesday?".
