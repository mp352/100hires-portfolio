## Topic: Programmatic Outbound via Clay
- Don't just scrape generic data from LinkedIn. Use Clay to cross-reference data from multiple sources (BuiltWith, Crunchbase, Github).
- Double-verify every single email using tools like Debounce or NeverBounce before launching a campaign to keep bounce rates strictly under 2%.
- Filter targets by triggers (e.g., "Company just hired a new Head of Sales").
