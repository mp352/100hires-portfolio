## Topic: Outbound Deliverability & Email Warmup
- Avoid using your primary domain for cold outreach. Always set up 3-5 lookalike domains (e.g., get[company].com).
- Limit sending volume to a maximum of 30-50 cold emails per day per inbox to stay under the radar of spam filters.
- Ensure custom tracking domains are configured to isolate your sender reputation.
